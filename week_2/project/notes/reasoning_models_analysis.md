Q-Star, also known as Strawberry, is a reasoning engine developed by OpenAI that aims to improve the reasoning capabilities of large language models (LLMs). Unlike standard next-token prediction, which relies on predicting the next word in a sequence, Q-Star uses test-time compute and reinforcement learning to enable more complex reasoning. This approach allows the model to use additional computational resources at test time to generate more accurate and informative responses. Q-Star has been shown to demonstrate human-like reasoning skills, enabling it to generate answers to queries that require more than just simple next-token prediction.
